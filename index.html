About Us

1967 A year that saw the birth of our company, Industrial Plastics, which started with Epoxy Insulator & Busbar support, Bakelite & Rubber products. As the company grew from strength to strength, to cater to the growing demand we diversified into manufacturing of Plastic components.

Over the years we have successfully augmented our plant with state-of-the art technology and equipment to ensure that our manufacturing and quality standards are as per international norms. Especially in times of increasing globalisation, responsible business becomes a central requirement – but also a duty – for the next generation

Working with customers to understand their needs is high on our priority and provides the best opportunity to achieve the best solution for each different application. Our manufacturing is formulated to provide you with the very best choice of system that will add value to your product and assist you in your business growth by focussing our attention to your requirements, thus giving your product a competitive advantage.
